Cosmic Encounters

A strategy board game for 3-5 players, ages 12+, with an average playtime of 60-120 minutes.

Description

In Cosmic Encounters, players assume the roles of alien species vying for control of the galaxy. Each player has unique powers and abilities, leading to asymmetrical gameplay and unpredictable interactions. The goal is to establish colonies on other players' home planets through negotiation, alliances, and strategic use of cards. The game is known for its high level of player interaction, bluffing, and tactical decision-making.
